Bot Commands

Commands

Simple slash commands to interact with Gift Bot.

/drop
Start a gift drop in the channel
Admins can manually trigger a gift drop for the server.
/wheel
Spin the lucky wheel
Requires 200 messages. Your streak determines your luck percentage.
/streak
Check your current streak
View your message streak and luck percentage for the wheel.
/leaderboard
View the top gift grabbers
See who has the most gifts and highest streaks in the server.
/profile
View your gift profile
See your total gifts, streak, messages, and luck stats.
/help
Get help and bot info
Shows all available commands and how to use them.
